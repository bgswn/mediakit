Meet the Creator.

Cinematic AI · Storytelling · Culture

I'm Muhammad Bobby Bangsawan — a creative storyteller, AI visual director, and digital entrepreneur from Indonesia. I specialize in crafting cinematic AI-generated content that blends emotion, culture, mythology, and modern visual aesthetics into immersive storytelling experiences.

My work spans AI filmmaking, cinematic prompt engineering, branded visual concepts, and social-first content creation. From historical dramas and folklore-inspired narratives to stylized music videos and surreal visual campaigns, I focus on creating stories that feel visually striking, emotionally engaging, and culturally memorable.
